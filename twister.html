TWISTER

Players face each other from opposite ends of the mat. The referee spins the spinner, then calls out the color. One participant from each couple picks a chit of body part and the opposite team player must then try to follow the directions. Each couple gets a lifeline in which player can loose the game or replace him/herself with the one picking up the chit. Elimination: Any player who falls, or touches the mat with an elbow or knee, is immediately out of the game. The last player left in the game is the winner!
