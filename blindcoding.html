Introduction

Being one of the biggest events of Technovision and one of its kind , Code Mania will not only test your programming skills but your passion towards programming.
It would be the ultimate test of your concentration skills with every round of it . There will 3 round with each round programs will become harder and harder. So do you think you are a good programmer ? if so come test your skills

Rounds

Round 1: It would be simple debugging test. Where you will given 2 programs were you just need to debug the errors.

Round 2: This round would be more harder then previous one. In this round you have to debug errors as well correct the logic.

Round 3:This round would be little tricky. Here you will be given piece of program and you just need to type it and also screen will be off while your typing. participants with least error will be the winner.

Rules

  1. You can choose either C++ or Java language only .
  2. Time limit will be provided in each round.. within that time you need to complete the given task.
  3. Participants are not allowed to bring any media devices such as pen drives, CD, DVD etc.
  4. Cell phones must be switched off.
