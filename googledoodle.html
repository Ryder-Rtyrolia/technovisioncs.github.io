Google Doodle

Trace the correct location and win the price. This is a single player game. The participant will be shown a particular image of any place. He/she has to find the location accordingly by activating their own phone data. The first 5 participants to find the answer will be eligible for level 2. In level 2 the first person to find the answer wins the game.

Entry fees: Rs.20/-
Winning prize: Rs.200/-
