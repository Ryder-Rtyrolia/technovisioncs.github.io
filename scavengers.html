Scavengers Hunt

Computer Scavenger Hunt A computer scavenger hunt is a great way to get everyone involved to using the computer and finding important sites on the internet or notepads and clues in laptops and decoding them. It is a fun way to start exploring as it helps to take away the feeling of being overwhelmed by something new. Materials:

  1. 5 laptops with 5 pen drives of various colours storing hidden clues in them.
  2. A reference board with rules and pointers
  3. A prepared scavenger hunt list.
Setup:
  1. Set 5 laptops for the game
  2. Set up notepads saved in pen drives or print our Blank Lists and fill in your own things to hunt in the laptops with help of technical clues.
  3. Then hide the items, or choose things that will be easy enough to find in the laptop (but not too easy!), such as each pen drive has information loaded which has a link to the next pen drive and so on until the last pen drive which has the ultimate login page in it and can be logged in by the password and login id mentioned in any of the previous pen drives.
  4. The running timer is the main loop . let your players (or teams) find all of the Clues and link them.
  5. Least errors least time required.
  6. The first ones to decode everything and login successfully will be the winner
Rules:
  1. The participants should have a sound technical knowledge.
  2. Each Team will comprise of 5 members.
  3. The team members cannot change their group.
  4. The team will try to find out solution of 1stquestion which will give clue of next place.
  5. Only one team member can access laptop. The team which will find the final login page in least amount of time will be awarded with winner certificate and a handsome cash prize of INR 2000.Runner up team will also get certificates. Procedure:
  6. Tell team members they are being sent on an adventurous scavenger hunt.
  7. They are not to return until they have collected all of the desired information.
  8. Hand out the laptop and let them search the right pen drive, and let the fun begin.
  9. Have students load the laptops with pen drives and begin using their resources to find the answers.
Note:
  1. Time plays the major role.
  2. Team members can't be swapped

Entry : Team of 5
Fee : Rs.250/team
